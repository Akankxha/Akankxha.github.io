Akansha Srivastava is a computational biologist and bioinformatics researcher. She received her PhD in Bioinformatics from IIIT Hyderabad (2023). Her research focuses on systems-level analysis of cancer metabolism and integrative genomics — combining transcriptomics with genome-scale metabolic networks to identify metabolic subtypes, biomarkers, and therapeutic opportunities.

Research interests
======
- Cancer systems biology and metabolic network analysis
- Transcriptome-driven modeling of metabolism
- Integrative analysis of genomics, copy-number and mutation data
- Machine learning and interpretable models for biomedical data
